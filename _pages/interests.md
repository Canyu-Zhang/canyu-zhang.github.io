## 🎤 A Cappella

I am a member of NYU Shanghai’s Octave Fusion A Cappella Choir, where I have had the chance to sing different voice parts including Tenor 1, Alto 2, Alto 1, and Soprano 1. Singing a cappella — creating music purely with voices — is a special experience for me. When rehearsing or performing with the group, I often feel completely immersed in the music, and all the stress and worries of the day simply fade away. The choir has become a warm and inspiring musical family for me.
